Crystal Collector Game!

1) You will be given a random number at the start of every game.


2) There are four crystals to choose from: Blue, Red, Yellow and Green and each crystal contains a certain number value (hidden)


3) By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


4) You win the game by matching your total score to the random number given in the beginning of game, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number given.


5) The crystals values will change everytime the game restarts.
